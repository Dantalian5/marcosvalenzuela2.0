About Me

Hey! I’m Marcos Valenzuela, a web developer based in Italy, a coding enthusiast with a love for manga, scale rockets, motorcycles, and robotics.

With a solid foundation in mathematics and science, I enjoy finding creative and practical solutions in web development and programming in general. I’m passionate about creating applications and websites that are both visually appealing and functional, always aiming to provide a smooth user experience. If you’re looking for someone eager to grow alongside your project, here I am! I love being part of new adventures and using my skills to bring your ideas to life in the digital world. Shall we talk? I’m ready to help bring your vision to reality.
